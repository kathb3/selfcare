about me

Hi, I'm Kathleen, and I'm an intern at Code Tenderloin. The internship session may have already ended when you're reading this, but I enjoyed learning about web development and soft skills. I made this website to help others either relax, cope, or cheer up when their day has gone wrong. Even if your day's been great, it's good to calm down and take a deep breath and notice the surroundings and the world.
